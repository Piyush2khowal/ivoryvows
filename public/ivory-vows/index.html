FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our digital invitations and services.

What is a digital invitation?

A digital invitation is a beautifully designed invite shared via WhatsApp, Instagram, Email, or SMS. It is a modern, eco-friendly, and convenient alternative to printed invitations.

What types of invitations do you create?

We create personalized invitations for Weddings, Engagements, Save the Date, Mehendi, Haldi, Sangeet, Reception, Bridal Shower, Baby Shower, Birthday Parties, Housewarming, Anniversaries, and Corporate Events.

Can I customize my invitation?

Yes! Every invitation is fully personalized with your names, event details, colors, fonts, photos, music, and theme to match your celebration perfectly.

How long does it take to receive my invitation?

Our standard delivery time is 3–7 business days depending on the design and customization. Express delivery is also available for urgent orders.

Can I make changes after receiving the first draft?

Absolutely! We offer revision rounds to ensure your invitation looks exactly the way you imagined.

Will my invitation work on Android and iPhone?

Yes. Our invitations work perfectly on Android phones, iPhones, tablets, and computers.

How will I receive my invitation?

Your invitation will be delivered as a shareable digital link and/or video file, making it easy to send through WhatsApp, Email, Instagram, Facebook, and other platforms.

Can you match my wedding theme or color palette?

Of course! We design invitations that perfectly match your wedding colors, décor, and overall event theme.

Can I add background music?

Yes! You can choose your favorite song or instrumental music, and we'll add it to your invitation (subject to licensing and compatibility).

Can I include Google Maps directions?

Yes. We can add clickable Google Maps links so your guests can easily find your venue.

Can I order invitations for multiple events?

Yes! We offer matching invitation suites for all your wedding events to maintain a beautiful and consistent look.

What information do I need to provide?

We need the couple's names, event details, date & time, venue, photos (optional), preferred theme or colors, RSVP details, and any special message.

Is payment required before work begins?

Yes. An advance payment is required to confirm your booking and begin designing your personalized invitation.

How can I place an order?

Simply contact us through our website or WhatsApp, share your event details, choose your preferred design, and we'll take care of the rest.

How much does a personalized digital invitation cost?

Pricing depends on the design, features, and level of customization. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your requirements.
